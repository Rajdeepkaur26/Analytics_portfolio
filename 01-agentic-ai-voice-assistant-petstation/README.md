## Project Overview
This project documents the design of an **agentic AI voice assistant** created as a business-facing feasibility solution for *PlayStayTion Pet Resort*. The goal was to automate high-volume inbound customer calls related to bookings, FAQs, and general inquiries while maintaining service quality, control, and governance.

The solution was designed using **Voiceflow** and **Twilio** during an active subscription period and presented to business stakeholders as a conceptual and functional prototype.

---

## Business Problem
PlayStayTion Pet Resort receives frequent inbound calls for:
- Booking inquiries
- Operating hours and service FAQs
- Basic customer support questions

Manual call handling created:
- Operational bottlenecks
- Inconsistent responses
- Limited scalability during peak periods

---

## Solution Design
The proposed solution uses an **agentic AI workflow** that:
- Classifies caller intent
- Retrieves accurate responses using **retrieval-augmented generation (RAG)**
- Escalates to a human agent when confidence is low or requests are complex

Key components:
- Intent classification and routing
- Knowledge grounding via structured business data
- Human-in-the-loop escalation
- Call logging and analytics for continuous improvement

## Governance & Risk Considerations

- Escalation rules to avoid automation overreach
- Transparency in AI-driven customer interactions
- Responsible AI usage in customer-facing systems
